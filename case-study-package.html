What this case study marketing service includes

The Case Study Package is designed to help specialist businesses package real work into something commercially useful. Instead of leaving strong delivery hidden in project folders, the service turns it into a case study asset that supports marketing, sales conversations and follow-up.

Deliverables

  • Project briefing, interview or story capture
  • One full written case study
  • Short-form version for sales use
  • LinkedIn adaptation or promotional cut-down
  • Quote and testimonial extraction
  • One round of revisions

Why it matters

Case studies help specialist businesses build trust faster. They give buyers something concrete to understand, and they give sales teams proof they can reuse in proposals, follow-up emails and commercial conversations.

For technical businesses especially, a strong case study can be the difference between sounding capable and looking proven.

How the process works

Mount Marketing starts with a project briefing or interview, then shapes the strongest commercial angle from the work. That story is turned into a polished case study, then adapted into shorter assets so the same project can support more than one marketing channel.

The aim is simple: make your best work easier to understand, easier to sell and easier to reuse.
