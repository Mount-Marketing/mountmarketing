FAQs

Questions specialist businesses ask before choosing a marketing agency.

Clear answers about case studies, campaigns, content support and how Mount Marketing works.

What kind of businesses does Mount Marketing work with?

Mount Marketing works best with specialist businesses, technical products and service-led companies that need clearer positioning, stronger marketing assets and commercially useful content. The work is based in Yorkshire and supports businesses across the UK.

Is Mount Marketing a marketing agency or outside support?

Mount Marketing works like a small specialist marketing agency and senior support partner. That means focused strategic and execution support without the overhead of a larger in-house team or broad generalist agency.

Can Mount Marketing create case studies from scratch?

Yes. The Case Study Package covers briefing, interviews, structure, writing and repurposing into sales-ready case studies, quotes and supporting marketing assets.

Do you support campaigns and launches?

Yes. The Campaign Support Package is designed for launches, events, webinars, partnerships and other commercial moments that need clear messaging, landing page copy, email content and campaign structure.

Do you offer ongoing marketing support?

Yes. The Content Support Subscription provides monthly content planning, regular LinkedIn content, longer-form assets and light strategic marketing input for businesses that need steadier visibility.

Do you only work in Yorkshire?

Mount Marketing is based in Yorkshire and works across the United Kingdom. Yorkshire is a strong regional focus, but support is not limited to one area.
