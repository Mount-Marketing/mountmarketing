Services

Case Study Package

Turn completed work into a high-trust sales asset, from interview and story development through to polished written, visual and social-ready content.

Includes

  • Project briefing, interview or story capture
  • One full written case study
  • Short-form versions for sales and social use
  • Quote, testimonial and trust-statement adaptations
  • Optional visual support for images, video and design
  • One round of revisions
Ideal for

Businesses with strong completed work that need it packaged into credible proof that can support trust, sales conversations and buying decisions.

Campaign Support Package

Focused support for launches, events, webinars, partnerships, approvals, certifications and other important commercial moments.

Includes

  • Campaign messaging
  • Campaign structure and content recommendations
  • LinkedIn copy
  • Email or newsletter copy
  • Website or landing page copy
  • Post-campaign recommendations
Ideal for

Businesses that need short-term marketing support around a specific project, launch or event.

Content Support Subscription

Ongoing content support for specialist businesses that need regular, credible marketing output without bringing everything in-house.

Includes

  • Monthly content planning
  • Regular LinkedIn content
  • One longer-form content asset or case study adaptation
  • Monthly review call
  • Light strategic input where needed
Ideal for

Businesses that want steady monthly marketing support without the overhead of a larger agency.
