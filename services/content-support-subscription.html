What this monthly content support service includes

The Content Support Subscription is designed for specialist businesses that need regular marketing output without taking everything in-house. It combines planning, content creation, case study adaptation and light strategic support so the business can show up more consistently.

Deliverables

  • Monthly content planning
  • Regular LinkedIn content
  • One longer-form content asset or case study adaptation
  • Monthly review call
  • Light strategic marketing input where needed

Why it matters

Many specialist businesses have valuable expertise but no consistent rhythm for turning that expertise into useful marketing. This package creates a repeatable structure, which makes it easier to stay visible, support sales activity and build credibility over time.

It is especially useful when there is no appetite for a larger agency retainer or permanent in-house marketing hire.

How the process works

Mount Marketing works from your priorities, recent commercial activity and the technical knowledge already inside the business. That gets turned into a practical monthly plan, regular content and follow-up assets the team can keep using beyond the first post or campaign.

The focus is steady, credible marketing support rather than content for content's sake.
